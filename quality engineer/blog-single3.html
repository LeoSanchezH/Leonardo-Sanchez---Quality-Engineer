Play to Earn: How Game Testing Can Help You Make Money on the Side

  • Leonardo Sanchez
  • API testing

Are you tired of mindlessly scrolling through your phone or playing the same video game over and over again? What if I told you that you could get paid to play video games on your phone, console, or computer? That’s right, game testing is a legitimate way to earn some extra cash while doing something you love. As a game tester , you’ll get paid to play video games and provide feedback to developers to help them improve the game. It’s a win-win situation — you get to play video games and earn money at the same time. This is a side hustle that I have done many times myself and I find it to pay fairly well (usually $150/hour and up). My experience was very fun and it involved playing a casino-type game and giving feedback about the layout, characters, music, and prizes of the game.

Game testing involves playing video games and providing feedback to developers on bugs, glitches, and other issues. Game testers typically play through different levels of the game and provide feedback on the game’s controls, graphics, and overall gameplay experience.

Game testers are an essential part of the game development process, as they help developers catch bugs and other issues before the game is released to the public. Game testers also provide valuable feedback on the game’s mechanics, which can help developers make changes to improve the game’s overall experience.
